How did I get here as a front-end developer.

First Part - The Books

Since I was a kid, I love to think about the possible ways to solve problems that I faced in my daily life. At that time, it was usaually about books.

I like to collect books, and I'd want them to be clean, never ever be folded, better not have any dust on them.

So I would think.. I want a super strong vent installed in front of the bookshelf to prevent dust from getting in, so the shelf will become a dust-free space; I want a bookshelf + suitcase combination so the books will sit safe and sound in the case while being transported.

These are the stuffs I never know how to build, even now.
